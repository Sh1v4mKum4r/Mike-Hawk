RemD

Welcome to the AI Virtual Painter! This application allows you to paint on a virtual canvas using hand gestures captured by your webcam.

Features:

  • Hand Tracking: Utilizes MediaPipe library for real-time hand tracking, enabling interaction with the virtual canvas.
  • Color Selection: Press keys 'r', 'b', 'g', 'c', 'm', 'y' to change colors (red, blue, green, cyan, magenta, yellow, respectively), and 'k' for the eraser.
  • Thickness Adjustment: Press keys '1' to '5' to adjust drawing thickness.
  • Clear Canvas: Detects a pinch gesture between thumb and index finger to clear the canvas.
  • Screenshot Capture: Press the 's' key to capture and save a screenshot of the canvas.

Start painting by moving your hand in front of the webcam. Have fun!
